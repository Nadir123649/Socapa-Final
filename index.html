Year-round Course Offerings

City Cinema classes run through the academic year and over the summer, offering points of entry into the program at any time.

10-Week

Fall & Spring Semester Classes

Our fall and winter/spring semester classes run over the course of 10 weeks, wherein students will attend two, 2-hour classes per week that run weekday evenings and/or weekends to accommodate high school schedules. There will be a couple of full weekend days towards the end of each course for film shoots and editing labs.
